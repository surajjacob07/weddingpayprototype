Module 01
Wedding Registry
The couple sets up gift card categories during onboarding. Guests visit the registry, pick a category, choose a brand, and purchase a gift card directly. Every gift card lands in the couple's vault with its redemption code. Give Forward is an optional add-on guests can choose at checkout.
6 curated categories — Home, Gold & Jewellery, Travel, Entertainment, Wellness, Fashion
50+ Indian gift card brands mapped to each category
Variable denominations — guest enters any amount from ₹100 to ₹50,000
Gift Card Vault — couple sees every card with redemption code (tap to reveal)
Give Forward — optional add-on at checkout, guest chooses their % on top
Payment via CC, UPI, Debit, Net Banking — owned by aggregator's gateway
